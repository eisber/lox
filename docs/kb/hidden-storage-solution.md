## Brief: I need a hidden storage solution.

A lot of people have a safe in their homes. They hide them in a variety of places, in a wardrobe, under a desk, in the loft etc. However, a safe screams “there’s valuable or important things in here”. A better option would be to have a hidden storage solution which could be hidden within a wall, for example.

With Loxone, you can implement intelligent, safe and intuitive access control to your hidden storage solution. The NFC Code Touch is the perfect device for this – allowing you to access your prized possessions with a PIN code, NFC Key Fob or even an NFC Tag.

## Solution: Using Loxone to create a hidden storage solution.

You simply need a hidden storage solution which has a motorised lock which is then connected to the Minisever. Then the NFC Code Touch can be used to gain entry either with a PIN code or an [NFC Tag](https://shop.loxone.com/enuk/nfc-smart-tags.html)/[Fob](https://shop.loxone.com/enuk/nfc-key-fob-set.html) – or both if you want to be extra safe and enable two-factor authentication.

## Why you and your customer should consider setting up a hidden storage solution?

By setting up an intelligent hidden storage solution, you can safely store important documents, valuable possessions or whatever else you want. Then Loxone will give you an intelligent way to access this compartment.

Burglars are, unfortunately, constantly developing their skills – which may result in them being able to find a way into a traditional safe. However, with a secret compartment, they will never even find your items.
