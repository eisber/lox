## OVERVIEW

Spotlights are very commonly used in our homes for areas where we want plenty of even light throughout a room with the minimal intrusion into the room that is not possible with a pendant. When installed it appears to have light shining from a hole in the ceiling, concentrating the light in a downward direction as a broad floodlight or narrow spotlight. Recessed lighting offers the advantages of being a hidden light source and energy efficiency.
