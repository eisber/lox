# Trigger Functions with QR Codes

Source: https://www.loxone.com/enen/kb/qr-codes/

---

QR Codes can be used to trigger functions.

The functions can be executed with any smartphone that can scan QR Codes and where the Miniserver is included in the App. In addition to the Web Interface/Apps permission, the user must also have the [right](https://www.loxone.com/help/usermanagement/) for the corresponding function.

## Generate QR Codes

Open the Loxone App and log in.

Via the settings you can select the menu item “Generate QR Code”.

In the following window you can define the desired function for the QR Code.

![GenerateQRCode menu](https://www.loxone.com/enen/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2016/10/GenerateQRCode_menu.png)

Now execute the switching operation or navigate to the desired position in the App.

The QR Code is generated by clicking on the QR icon (bottom right) and can be saved in the next step and forwarded or printed out.

![SaveQRCode](https://www.loxone.com/enen/wp-content/uploads/sites/3/2016/10/SaveQRCode.png)
