## OVERVIEW

When looking to utilise a current pendant light fittings in a project often these are 230V fittings. There are two ways these types of fittings can be controlled by Loxone, dimmed and switched.

Depending on the installation situation the Loxone Nano I/O Air will give you switched control of two circuits as this product has two separate relays. If the circuit is looking to be dimmed then we use a technology called phase dimming and the product required to do this is the Nano Dimmer Air.

Please select which type of control you require below.
