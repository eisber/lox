## Brief: I want to charge electric vehicles as cheaply as possible

In a world where sustainability and energy efficiency are becoming increasingly important, electric vehicle owners face the challenge of charging their vehicles in a cost-effective and environmentally friendly way.

## Solution: Set situation-dependent charging modes with the Loxone Wallbox

The [Loxone Wallbox](https://shop.loxone.com/enen/catalogsearch/result/?q=wallbox+11kw) makes it easy to configure different charging modes that are perfectly tailored to the needs and circumstances of electric car owners. These can be easily switched using a [Touch Pure Flex](https://shop.loxone.com/enen/catalogsearch/result/?q=touch+pure+flex+wallbox), which can be installed in the Wallbox. The resulting energy flows can be easily metered via the [Energy Meter 3-Phase Tree](https://shop.loxone.com/enen/energy-meter-3-phase-tree.html) and visualized with the [Energy Flow Monitor](https://www.loxone.com/enen/kb/energy-flow-monitor/) function block. An example configuration could look like this:

**Full Power:** This charging mode is used when the electric vehicle needs to be charged as quickly as possible. In the case of the [Loxone Wallbox](https://shop.loxone.com/enen/catalogsearch/result/?q=wallbox+11kw), charging is possible with up to 11kW.

**Comfort:** Here, charging takes place over a longer period of time, such as from 21:00-06:00, with lower power. In the example configuration, this is 5kW. This is battery-friendly charging.

**Eco:** This mode means that the electric vehicle is only charged when excess power is available – e.g. from a PV system.
