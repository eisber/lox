# LED Ceiling Spot WW Tree

Source: https://www.loxone.com/enen/kb/led-ceiling-spot-ww/

---

This product will not be available for purchase after 15th June 2019.
There is a Gen. 2 version of the LED Spot designed specifically for the UK.
The [LED Spot RGBW Tree White (UK)](https://shop.loxone.com/enuk/led-spot-rgbw-tree-white-uk.html) is fire-rated and offers directional warm white light and diffuse coloured light to provide an incomparable lighting experience in a Real Smart Home.

## TECHNICAL DATA
- LED Type: WW LED
- Colour Temperature: 3000 Kelvin Warm White
- Light Output: 804 Lumen
- Dimming: 1 – 100% PWM
- PWM Signal: 125 – 500Hz
- Beam Angle 120°
- Power Supply: 24VDC SELV(-10%…+5%)
- Power Consumption:

Warm White: 9.4W
- Operating Temperature: 0° – 50°C
- Protection: IP 20
- Colour Rendering Index (CRI): Ra: 80 (Typically 82)
- Surface Temperature: 60°C (at room temperature)
- Dimensions:

Diameter: 80mm
- Height: 70mm
- Reverse Voltage Protection
- Grouped via hardware (wiring)
- Wiring type: RGBW Tree/Air Dimmer

## CONNECTION

The LED Ceiling Spot WW has a 24V and Negative connector built in.
