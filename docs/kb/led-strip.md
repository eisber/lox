## OVERVIEW

LED strip lighting is becoming more widely used throughout the home to create smooth background or accent lighting. The thousands of colour options that can be created will set the mood for any occasion. With a high lumen output and a very long life span on the LED diodes, they are becoming more popular for use around TVs, under cabinets/plinth lighting, stairs, bathrooms and even pools!
