## OVERVIEW

In new build homes there are times where using pendant or other light fittings is preferable over downlights or LED Strips. When planning for 230V switched or dimmed lighting circuits, it is normally most practical to star wire back to a central point as this will give you the most flexibility and be as future proof as possible should anything change later down the line. I.e change of a fitting or upgrade to 24V lighting system.
