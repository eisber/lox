The LED Spot WW PWM is available in different variants:

[**Datasheet LED Ceiling Spot WW PWM**](https://www.loxone.com/wp-content/uploads/datasheets/Datasheet_LEDCeilingSpotWW-PWM_100505,100506.pdf)

These spots are supplied with a nominal voltage of 24V DC and can be dimmed by PWM.

Loxone devices with RGBW or PWM outputs are suitable for this:

[RGBW Dimmer Tree](https://www.loxone.com/help/rgbw-dimmer-tree)

[RGBW Dimmer Air](https://www.loxone.com/help/rgbw-dimmer-air)

[Multi Extension Air](https://www.loxone.com/help/multi-air)

[Nano Motor Controller Tree](https://www.loxone.com/help/nano-motor-controller-tree)

[Nano Motor Controller Air](https://www.loxone.com/help/nano-motor-controller-air)

If dimming is not required, the supply line to the spots can also simply be switched via a relay contact.

### Safety Instructions

The installation must be carried out by a qualified technician in accordance with all applicable regulations

The recessed spots are UL 1598 Type IC certified, making them suitable for installation on flammable surfaces.
