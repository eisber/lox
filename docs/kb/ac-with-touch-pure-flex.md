### Simple control of air conditioning systems via a central control unit and app integration.

With the specially developed **Touch Pure Flex,** individual functions can be easily implemented.

In this project, the Touch Pure Flex Battery Air was used, which brings the control of the air conditioning system to a simple and clear button.

The Touch Pure Flex Battery Air **replaces** the **conventional thermostat** and enables **complete, intuitive operation** of the air conditioning system – wireless, battery-operated and seamlessly integrated into the Loxone system.

The AC Unit Controller can be operated directly from the device via the API.

**Functions at a glance:**
- On/off switch
- Silent Mode
- Adjustable target temperature (18–32 °C in 1 °C increments)
- Selection of operating mode: Auto, Heating, Cooling, Dry, Fan
- Fan speed control (up to 7 levels)
- Adjustment of the airflow direction (auto, swivel function, fixed positions)

An **integrated humidity sensor** automatically activates the dry function when the humidity is above 65% during cooling mode and switches back to Auto when the humidity falls below 55%.

This ensures that the desired temperature is reached more quickly and noticeably increases comfort.
