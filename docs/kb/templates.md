# Templates

Source: https://www.loxone.com/enen/kb/templates/

---

Templates can be used to facilitate the integration of devices or communication via interfaces.

Predefined templates from the [Loxone Library](https://library.loxone.com/) can be used or individual templates can be created and shared.
