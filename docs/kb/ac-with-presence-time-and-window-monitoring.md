### Automatic and energy-efficient air conditioning control – dependent on presence, schedule and window status.

The air conditioning (AC) should only be active if **all of** the following conditions are met:
- **Presence** in the room detected
- current time is **within the weekly schedule**
- **all windows and doors** are closed

The air conditioning is only activated if a presence is detected within the timer’s defined time window. The time windows can be flexibly adjusted in the app.

If a window or door is opened, the air conditioning automatically pauses and remains deactivated until all openings are closed again.
