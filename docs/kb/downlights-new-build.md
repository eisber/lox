## OVERVIEW

When designing lighting it is important to know at the outset what kind of light will be used, the technical specifications of it and how it is to be controlled.

## RECCOMENDATION

Here at Loxone we suggest using 24V DC spots when it comes to new build as the hardware costs are much less, especially when looking at dimming. There are also some other benefits to using 24V lighting which you’ll find detailed on the next page.
