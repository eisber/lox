## OVERVIEW

Pendants are one of the most common form of lighting found in our homes. In their base form they are centrally placed in a room to shine light out to all areas, normally the lamp/bulb is attached using a generic fitting to the end of a cable or wire. They are typically the cheapest and simplest form of main lighting in a room.

Pendants are not always a simple plain fitting as they can take many forms and there are some beautiful designs on the market today.
