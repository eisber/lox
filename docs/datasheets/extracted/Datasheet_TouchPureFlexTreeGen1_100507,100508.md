About the product
The Touch Pure Flex Tree is your 100% personalized control device with up to 12 freely definable and positionable touch points. With the integrated LED matrix display, as well as 3 status LEDs, menus, values, texts and states can be displayed. In addition, the Touch Pure Flex Tree also serves as a Room Comfort Sensor thanks to the integrated temperature and humidity sensor.

Zudem dient der Touch Pure Flex Tree dank integriertem Temperatur- und Feuchtesensor zugleich als Raumklimasensor.
