Valve Actuator Air
Part Nr: 100603
About the product
The Loxone Valve Actuator Air is a wireless motorized actuator. By using common adapter rings, it can be mounted on a wide range of valves, which are typically used for in-floor heating and radiators.
Features:
· Push force of 100N
· Interchangeable Adapter
