About the product
The Loxone Touch for Nano has five touch points to operate the most important functions of a room. The large central touch zone is ideal for controlling lighting, while the corner zones are suitable for controlling music and blinds. The Loxone button standard is used here. It is controlled via the Nano Relay Air.
