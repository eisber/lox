About the product
The Remote Air is a flexible and mobile control device which brings all essential functions of an intelligent building into your hand. The well known Loxone switch standard as well as additional buttons for audio control. All that in a high-class design.
Features:
. 12 touch buttons, including 5 following the Loxone switch standard
· Predefined buttons for lighting, shading, and audio
. 3 freely configurable buttons
· High-quality casing
· Operates with two AAA batteries
