Loxone Tree Cable Eca (200m) Part No: 100637
About the product
The Loxone Tree Cable contains wire pairs for wiring DC power supply and communication of Tree devices. A separate wire pair with 1.5mm2 (16 AWG) cross-section is also included to supply devices with higher current requirements.
