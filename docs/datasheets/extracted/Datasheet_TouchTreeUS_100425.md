About the product
The Loxone Touch Tree has five touch points to operate the most important functions of a room. The large central touch zone is ideal for controlling lighting, while the corner zones are suitable for controlling music and blinds. The Loxone button standard is used here. A sensor for measuring temperature and humidity is also integrated.
