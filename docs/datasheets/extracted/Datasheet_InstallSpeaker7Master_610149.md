About the product
The Install Speaker 7 Master impresses with its outstanding audio qualities and its numerous possible use cases. It is connected directly via Tree Turbo to an Audioserver or Miniserver Compact and is able to transmit the audio signal to a chain of up to 20 client speakers. In this way, it forms a flexible audio solution scalable to any building size.
Features:
· Full integration into the Loxone System
. countless features like text-to-speech, doorbell, alarms, or audio notifications
· Music Streaming via Bluetooth
